量子力学公式

以中科院811和陈鄂生黄皮为复习资料，总结的量子力学考试里需要记忆的公式，要么是必须记住的，要么是“反正这么常见写了不知道多少次你顺手背了呗”的常见结论。

1.积分

$\int_{0}^{\infty} x^{n} e^{-a x} d x=\frac{n !}{a^{n+1}}$

$\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} e^{-x^{2}} d x=\sqrt{\pi}$

$\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} e^{i m x} d x=2 \pi \delta(m)$

$\delta(\alpha x)=\frac{1}{|\alpha|} \delta(x)$

$\int_{0}^{\infty} x^{2 n} e^{-a x^{2}} d x=\frac{(2 n-1) ! !}{2^{n+1} a^{n}} \sqrt{\frac{\pi}{a}}$

$\int_{0}^{\pi} \sin (m x) \sin (n x) d x=\frac{\pi}{2} \delta_{mn}$

2.算符

${\left[\hat{x}{i}, \hat{P}{j}\right]=i \hbar \delta_{ i j}}$

${\left[\hat{L}{\alpha}, \hat{L}{\beta}\right]=\varepsilon_{\alpha \beta \gamma }i \hbar \hat{L}{\gamma} \quad\left[\hat{L}^{2}, \hat{L}{\alpha}\right]=0}$

$\left[\hat{L}{\alpha}, \hat{P}{\beta}\right]=\varepsilon_{\alpha \beta \gamma }i \hbar \hat{P}_{\gamma}$

$\left[\hat{L}{\alpha}, \hat{x}{\beta}\right]=\varepsilon_{\alpha \beta \gamma }i \hbar \hat{x}_{\gamma}$

$[\hat{P}, f(x)]=-i\hbar \frac{\partial f(x)}{\partial x}$

对定态有 $[\hat{x}, \hat{H}]=\frac{i \hbar}{\mu} \hat{P}$

$e^{\hat{A} } \hat{B}e^{-\hat{A}}=\hat{B}+[\hat{A}, \hat{B}]+\frac{1}{2 !}[\hat{A},[\hat{A}, \hat{B}]]+\frac{1}{3 !}[\hat{A},[\hat{A}, [\hat{A}, \hat{B}]]]+\dots$

空间平移： $e^{-i \hat{P}\left(x-x_{0}\right) / \hbar}\left|x_{0}\right\rangle=\left|x\right\rangle$

时间平移： $e^{-i \hat{H}\left(t-t_{0}\right) / \hbar}\left|t_{0}\right\rangle=\left|t\right\rangle$

空间旋转： $e^{-i \hat{L} \cdot \vec{n} \cdot\left(\varphi-\varphi_{0}\right) / \hbar}\left|\varphi_{0}\right\rangle=\left|\varphi\right\rangle$

几率流密度： $j \equiv \frac{i \hbar}{2 \mu}\left(\psi \nabla \psi^{}-\psi^{} \nabla \psi\right)$

3.一维定态

一维无限方势阱

$E_{n}=\frac{n^{2} \pi^{2} \hbar^{2}}{2 \mu a^{2}} ,\psi_{n}=\left{\begin{array}{ll} \sqrt{\frac{2}{a}} \sin \frac{n \pi x}{a} & 0<x<a \ 0 & x<0, x>a \end{array}\right.$

势垒 $V=V_{0} \quad(x&gt;0)$

当入射能量 $E&gt;V_0$ 时：

反射率 $R=\frac{(k-\alpha)^{2}}{(k+\alpha)^{2}}$ ，透射率 $T=1-R=\frac{4 k \alpha}{(k+\alpha)^{2}}$

$k=\sqrt{\frac{2 \mu E}{\hbar^{2}}} \quad \alpha=\sqrt{\frac{2\mu\left(E-V_{0}\right)}{\hbar^{2}}}$

当入射能量 $E&lt;V_0$ 时：

$R=1 \quad T=0$

$\delta$ 势边界条件 $V(x)=A \delta(a)$ ： $\psi^{\prime}\left(a^{+}\right)-\psi^{\prime}\left(a^{-}\right)=\frac{2 \mu A}{\hbar^{2}} \psi(a)$

平面转子：

$\hat{H}=\frac{\hat{L}_{z}^{2}}{2 I}=-\frac{\hbar^{2}}{2 I} \frac{d^{2}}{d \varphi^{2}}$

$\psi_{m}(\varphi)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi}} e^{\operatorname{im\varphi } }$ ，$m \in Z$

一维谐振子

$\begin{array}{l} V(x)=\frac{1}{2} \mu w^{2} x^{2} \quad E_{n}=\left(n+\frac{1}{2}\right) \hbar w \ \psi_{n}(x)=N_{n} e^{-\alpha^{2} x^{2} / 2} H_{n}(\alpha x) \ \alpha=\sqrt{\frac{\mu w}{\hbar}} \quad N_{n}=\sqrt{\frac{\alpha}{\sqrt{\pi} \cdot 2^{n} \cdot n !}} \ \begin{aligned} a & \equiv \sqrt{\frac{\mu w}{2 \hbar}}\left(\hat{x}+\frac{i}{\mu w} \hat{p}\right) \ a^{+} & \equiv \sqrt{\frac{\mu w}{2 \hbar}}\left(\hat{x}-\frac{i}{\mu w} \hat{p}\right) \ x &=\sqrt{\frac{\hbar}{2 m w}}\left(a^{\dagger}+a\right) \ \hat{p} &=i \sqrt{\frac{\mu w \hbar}{2}}\left(a^{\dagger}-a\right) \ x|n\rangle &=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} \alpha}\left(a^{\dagger}|n\rangle+a|n\rangle\right) \ &=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} \alpha}(\sqrt{n+1}|n+1\rangle+\sqrt{n}|n-1\rangle) \ \frac{d}{d x}|n\rangle &=\frac{d}{\sqrt{2}}\left(a|n\rangle-a^{\dagger}|n\rangle\right) \ &=\frac{\alpha}{\sqrt{2}}(\sqrt{n}|n-1\rangle-\sqrt{n+1}|n+1\rangle) \end{aligned} \end{array}$

最后两个代数解法给出的公式极为重要，谐振子出题能手算的大部分都是从这个角度出

4.角动量

升降算符： $\hat{L}{\pm} \equiv \hat{L}{x} \pm i \hat L_{y} \quad \hat{J}{\pm}\equiv \hat{J}{x} \pm i \hat J_{y}$

$\begin{array} \hat {J}{\pm}|j m\rangle=\sqrt{j(j+1)-m(m \pm 1)} \hbar|j, m \pm 1\rangle \ \hat{J}^{2}|j m\rangle=j(j+1) \hbar^{2}|j m\rangle \ \hat{J}{z}|j m\rangle=m \hbar|j m\rangle \end{array}$

$\begin{array}{l} \hat{L}{x}=\frac{\hbar}{2}\left(\begin{array}{lll} 0 & 1 & 0 \ 1 & 0 & 1 \ 0 & 1 & 0 \end{array}\right) \quad \hat{L{y}}=\frac{\hbar}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\begin{array}{ccc} 0 & -i & 0 \ i & 0 & -i \ 0 & i & 0 \end{array}\right) \quad \hat{L}_{z}=\hbar\left(\begin{array}{ccc} 1 & 0 & 0 \ 0 & 0 & 0 \ 0 & 0 & -1 \end{array}\right) \ \end{array}$

$l_x=\hbar,0,-\hbar$ 对应的本征矢为：

$\begin{array}{ll}\frac{1}{2}\left(\begin{array}{c} 1 \ \sqrt{2} \ 1 \end{array}\right) \quad \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\begin{array}{c} 1 \ 0 \ -1 \end{array}\right) \quad \frac{1}{2}\left(\begin{array}{c} 1 \ -\sqrt{2} \ 1 \end{array}\right) \ \end{array}$

$l_y=\hbar,0,-\hbar$ 对应的本征矢为：

$\frac{1}{2}\left(\begin{array}{c} 1 \ \sqrt{2} i \ -1 \end{array}\right) \quad \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\begin{array}{c} 1 \ 0 \ 1 \end{array}\right) \quad \frac{1}{2}\left(\begin{array}{c} 1 \ -\sqrt{2} i \ -1 \end{array}\right)$

5.表象

$\begin{array}{l} \psi(\vec{r}, t)=\frac{1}{(2 \pi \hbar)^{\frac{3}{2}}} \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} \varphi(\vec{p}, t) e^{i \vec{p} \cdot \vec{r} / \hbar} d^{3} \vec{p} \ \varphi(\vec{p}, t)=\frac{1}{(2 \pi \hbar)^{\frac{3}{2}}} \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} \psi(\vec{r}, t) e^{-i \vec{p} \cdot \vec{r} / \hbar} d^{3} \vec{r} \ i \hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \varphi(\vec{p}, t)=\frac{p^{2}}{2 \mu} \varphi(\vec{p}, t)+\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} V_{p p^{\prime}} \varphi\left(\overrightarrow{p^{\prime}}, t\right) d^{3} \overrightarrow{p^{\prime}}\ V_{p p^{\prime}}=\frac{1}{(2 \pi \hbar)^3}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} V(\vec{r}, t) e^{-i (\vec{p}-\vec{p^{\prime}}) \cdot \vec{r} / \hbar} d^{3} \vec{r} \end{array}$

维里定理： $2\langle\hat{T}\rangle_{n}=\langle\vec{r} \cdot \nabla V(\vec{r}^{2})\rangle_{n}$

势能是 $\vec {r}$ 的齐次函数 $\lambda^{s} V(\vec{r})$ 时： $\langle\hat{T}\rangle_{n}=\frac{s}{2}\langle\hat{V}\rangle_{n}$

F-H定理（对束缚定态， $\lambda$ 是参数）： $\frac{\partial E_{n}}{\partial \lambda}=\overline{\left(\frac{\partial \hat{H}}{\partial \lambda}\right)_{n}}$

海森堡绘景： $\frac{d \hat{F}{H}(t)}{d t}=\frac{1}{i \hbar}\left[\hat{F}{H}(t), \hat{H}_{H}(t)\right]$

力学量F平均值的速率： $\frac{d \bar{F}(t)}{d t}=\frac{\partial \bar{F}}{\partial t}+\frac{1}{i \hbar} \overline{[\hat{F}, \hat{H}]}$

6.三维定态

$\begin{array}{l} \psi(\vec{r})=R(r) Y_{l m}(\theta, \varphi)=\frac{u(r)}{r} Y_{l m}(\theta, \varphi) \ \frac{d^{2} R(r)}{d r^{2}}+\frac{2}{r} \frac{d R(r)}{d r}+\left(\frac{2 \mu}{\hbar^{2}}(E-V(r))-\frac{l(l+1)}{r^{2}}\right) \cdot R(r)=0 \ \frac{d^{2} u(r)}{d r^{2}}+\left(\frac{2 \mu}{\hbar^{2}}(E-V(r))-\frac{l(l+1)}{r^{2}}\right) u(r)=0 \quad U(0)=0 \end{array}$

类氢离子势： $V(r)=-\frac{Ze^2}{r}$

$E_{n}=-\frac{ Z^{2} e^{2}}{2 a n^{2}} \quad \psi_{n l m}(r)=R_{n l}(r) Y_{l m}(\theta, \varphi)$

$R_{nl}(r)=N_{nl}e ^ { - \frac { Z r } { n a } } ( \frac { 2 Z r } { n a } ) ^ { l } F (l+1-n, 2l+2, \frac{2 Z r}{n a})$

其中 $n,l,m\in Z,n\geq1,0\leq l\leq n-1,-l\leq m \leq l$

7.电磁场中的带电粒子

$\begin{array}{ll} \hat{H}=\frac{1}{2 \mu}\left(\hat{p}-\frac{q}{c} A(\vec{r}, t)\right)^{2}+q \Phi(\vec{r}, t) \ \hat{v}=\frac{1}{\mu}\left(\hat{p}-\frac{q}{c} \vec{A}\right) \end{array}$

常见磁矢势： $\begin{array}{ll} A=\left(-\frac{B}{2} y, \frac{B}{2} x, 0\right) \ A=(-B y, 0,0) \end{array}$

8.自旋与耦合

$\hat{M}{S}=-\frac{e}{\mu c} \hat{S} \quad \hat{S}^{2}=\hat{S}{x}^{2}+\hat{S}{y}^{2}+\hat{S}{z}^{2}=\frac{3}{4} \hbar^{2}$

$\hat{S}_z$ 表象下：

$\begin{array}{l} \hat{S}^{2}=\frac{3}{4} \hbar^{2}\left(\begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \ 0 & 1 \end{array}\right) \quad \hat S_{x}=\frac{\hbar}{2}\left(\begin{array}{ll} 0 & 1 \ 1 & 0 \end{array}\right) \ \hat S_{y}=\frac{\hbar}{2}\left(\begin{array}{cc} 0 & -i \ i & 0 \end{array}\right)\quad \hat S_{z}=\frac{\hbar}{2}\left(\begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \ 0 & -1 \end{array}\right) \end{array}$

对应的本征值和本征矢：

$\begin{array}{l} S_{z}=\frac{\hbar}{2} \quad \varphi_{+}=\left(\begin{array}{l} 1 \ 0 \end{array}\right) \quad S_{z}=-\frac{\hbar}{2} \quad \varphi_{-}=\left(\begin{array}{l} 0 \ 1 \end{array}\right)\ S_{x}=\frac{\hbar}{2} \quad \varphi_{+}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\begin{array}{l} 1 \ 1 \end{array}\right) \quad S_{x}=-\frac{\hbar}{2} \quad \varphi_{-}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\begin{array}{l} 1 \ -1 \end{array}\right)\ S_{y}=\frac{\hbar}{2} \quad \varphi_{+}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\begin{array}{l} 1 \ i \end{array}\right) \quad S_y=-\frac{\hbar}{2} \quad \varphi_{-}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\begin{array}{l} 1 \ -i \end{array}\right)\ \hat{S_{n}}=\hat{S} \cdot \vec{n}=\frac{\hbar}{2}\left(\begin{array}{cc} \cos \theta & \sin \theta e^{-i \varphi} \ \sin \theta e^{i \varphi} & -\cos \theta \end{array}\right)\ S_{n}=\frac{\hbar}{2} \quad \varphi_{+}=\left(\begin{array}{l} \cos \frac{\theta}{2} e^{-i \varphi / 2} \ \sin \frac{\theta}{2} e^{i \varphi / 2} \end{array}\right) \S_{n}=-\frac{\hbar}{2} \quad \varphi_{-}=\left(\begin{array}{l} -\sin\frac{\theta}{2} e^{-i \varphi / 2} \ \cos\frac{\theta}{2} e^{i \varphi / 2} \end{array}\right) \end{array}$

泡利矩阵：

$\begin{array}{l} \sigma_x=\left(\begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \ 1 & 0 \end{array}\right) \quad \sigma_y=\left(\begin{array}{cc} 0 & -i \ i & 0 \end{array}\right) \quad \sigma_z=\left(\begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \ 0 & -1 \end{array}\right) \ \sigma_{i}^{\dagger }=\sigma_{i}\quad \sigma_{i}^{2}=1 \ {\left[\sigma_{i}, \sigma_{j}\right]=2i\varepsilon_{ijk} \sigma_{k} } \ \left{\sigma_{i}, \sigma_{j}\right}=0 \quad \sigma_{i} \sigma_{j}=i \varepsilon_{i j k} \sigma_{k} \end{array}$

双电子自旋耦合：

$\begin{array}{l} \hat{S}{1}\cdot \hat{S}{2}=\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{S}^{2}-\frac{3}{2} \hbar^{2}\right) \ \hat{S}{1 z} \cdot \hat{S}{2 z}=\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{S}_{z}-\frac{\hbar^{2}}{2}\right) \end{array}$

电子总角动量：

$\begin{array}{l} \hat{J}=\hat{L}+\hat{S} \ \hat{J}^{2}=\hat{L}^{2}+\frac{3}{4} \hbar^{2}+2\hat{L} \cdot \hat{S}\ \hat{L} \cdot \hat{S}=\frac{1}{2}\left(\hat{J}^{2}-\hat{L}^{2}-\frac{3}{4} \hbar^{2}\right) \end{array}$

双1/2粒子耦合：

$\begin{array}{l} |11\rangle=\uparrow \uparrow \ |10\rangle=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(\uparrow \downarrow+\downarrow \uparrow) \ |1-1\rangle=\downarrow \downarrow \ |00\rangle=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(\uparrow \downarrow-\downarrow \uparrow) \end{array}$

注意三重态交换对称，单态交换反对称

9.近似方法

$\begin{array}{l} E_{n}^{(1)}=H_{m n}^{\prime} \quad E_{n}^{(2)}=\sum_{m \neq n} \frac{\left|H_{m n}^{\prime}\right|^{2}}{E_{n}^{(0)}-E_{m}^{(0)}} \ \psi_{n}^{(1)}=\sum_{m \neq n} \frac{H_{m n}^{\prime}}{E_{n}^{(0)}-E_{m}^{(0)}} \psi_{m}^{(0)} \ \end{array}$

定态简并微扰公式一样流程不同，记得算**零级波函数**即可

变分法：

$\begin{array}{l} E(\alpha)=\langle\psi(r, \alpha)|\hat{H}| \psi(r, \alpha)\rangle \ \frac{\partial E(\alpha)}{\partial \alpha}=0 \end{array}$ 求出后带入 $E(\alpha),\psi(\alpha)$

跃迁：

1* $H^{\prime}$ 含时间

$\begin{array}{l} W_{k \rightarrow m}(t)=\frac{1}{\hbar^{2}}\left|\int_{0}^{t} H_{m k}^{\prime}(t) e^{i \omega_{m k} t} d t\right|^{2} \ \omega_{m k}=\frac{E m-E_{k}}{\hbar} \ \end{array}$

2* $H^{\prime}$ 不显含时间

$W_{k \rightarrow m}=\frac{2 \pi}{\hbar}\left|H_{m k}^{\prime}(E)\right|^{2} \rho(E)$

$W$ 是k态到m态的跃迁速率， $\rho(E)$ 是能态密度

连续光照电偶极近似跃迁速率：

$\begin{array}{l} W_{k\rightarrow m}=\frac{4 \pi^{2} e^{2}}{3 \hbar^{2}} I\left(\left|\omega_{m k}\right|\right)\left|{r_{m k}}\right|^{2} \ \left|{r}{m k}\right|^{2}=\left|x{m k}\right|^{2}+\left|y_{m k}\right|^{2}+|z_{m k}|^{2} \end{array}$

电偶极跃迁选择定则为 $\Delta l =\pm 1 \quad\Delta m=0,\pm 1$

原子自发跃迁速率： $A_{k \rightarrow m}=\frac{4 e^{2} w^{3}{k m}}{3 \hbar c^{3}}\left|{r{m k}}\right|^{2}$

10.全同粒子

同一力场中的全同粒子体系：

fermion

$\psi_{f}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{N !}}\left|\begin{array}{cccc} \varphi_{1}\left(q_{1}\right) & \varphi_{1}\left(q_{2}\right) & \cdots & \varphi_{1}\left(q_{N}\right) \ \varphi_{2}\left(q_{1}\right) & \varphi_{2}\left(q_{2}\right) & \cdots & \varphi_{2}\left(q_{N}\right) \ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots \ \varphi_{N}\left(q_{1}\right) & \varphi_{N}\left(q_{2}\right) & \cdots & \varphi_{N}\left(q_{N}\right) \end{array}\right|$

boson

$\psi_{b}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{R}} \sum_{\alpha} \hat{P}{\alpha}\left(\varphi{1}\left(a_{1}\right) \varphi_{2}\left(a_{2}\right) \cdots \varphi_{n}\left(a_{n}\right)\right)$

这里的 $\hat P$ 是交换算符

对fermion体系有：

$\begin{array}{l} \left{a_{i}, a_{j}^\dagger\right}=\delta_{i j} \quad\left{a_{i}, a_{j}\right}=\left{a_{i}^{\dagger}, a_{j}^{\dagger}\right}=0 \ a_{i}\left|n_{i}\right\rangle=\sqrt{n_{i}}\left|n_{i}-1\right\rangle \ a_{i}{ }^{\dagger}\left|n_{i}\right\rangle=\sqrt{1-n_{i}}\left|n_{i}+1\right\rangle \ a_{i}\left|n_{1} n_{2} \cdots n_{i} \cdots\right\rangle=(-1)^{m} \sqrt{n_{i}}\left|n_{1} n_{2} \cdots n_{i}-1 \cdots\right\rangle \ a_{i}^{\dagger}\left|n_{1} n_{2} \cdots n_{i} \cdots\right\rangle=(-1)^{m} \sqrt{1-n_{i}}\left|n_{1} n_{2}\cdots n_{i}+1 \cdots\right\rangle \ m \equiv \sum_{\alpha=1}^{i-1} n_{\alpha} \end{array}$

对boson体系有：

$\begin{array}{l} [a_i, a_{j}^\dagger]=\delta_{i j} \quad[a_{i}, a_{j}]=[a_{i}^{\dagger}, a_{j}^{\dagger}]=0 \ a_{i}\left|n_{i}\right\rangle=\sqrt{n_{i}}\left|n_{i}-1\right\rangle \ a_{i}{ }^{\dagger}\left|n_{i}\right\rangle=\sqrt{1+n_{i}}\left|n_{i}+1\right\rangle \ a_{i}\left|n_{1} n_{2} \cdots n_{i} \cdots\right\rangle=\sqrt{n_{i}}\left|n_{1} n_{2} \cdots n_{i}-1 \cdots\right\rangle \ a_{i}^{\dagger}\left|n_{1} n_{2} \cdots n_{i} \cdots\right\rangle= \sqrt{1+n_{i}}\left|n_{1} n_{2}\cdots n_{i}+1 \cdots\right\rangle \ \end{array}$
